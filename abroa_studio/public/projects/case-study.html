Brand Identity & Web Design

Lumina Architecture

  • Client: Lumina Arch
  • Year: 2025
  • Services: Strategy, Identity, UI/UX, Development

Lumina creates spaces that inspire. They needed a digital presence that reflected their minimalist philosophy while showcasing their complex portfolio. We built a brand system that steps back, letting the architecture speak for itself.

The Challenge

The previous identity felt dated and cluttered, failing to communicate the premium nature of their residential projects. The website was difficult to navigate, burying their best work under layers of menus.

The Solution

We stripped everything back. A monochromatic palette allows the photography to pop. The new typography—Inter—is confident yet neutral. Navigation was simplified to three core paths: Projects, Studio, Contact.
